The Food Of Christmas

FRANCE

The trunks like cake Buchedenoel is the famous French Christmas gourmet. Before the invention of electricity, the French used a good firewood as a Christmas gift.


CANADA

The Christmas dinner in Canada has many similarities with its colonial ancestor, England, and the neighbours of the United States. The traditional Christmas dinner features stuffed turkey (flavoring), mashed potatoes, broth thick juice, cranberry sauce, vegetables, and raisin pudding as a dessert. Egg wine, a kind of milk - base mixed egg juice, sugar and other drinks, usually served with wine, is also very popular in the festival. The other Christmas meals including butter and cream cakes stuffing tower, according to tradition will be baked in day before Christmas and New Year reception for visiting various parties and friends on Christmas day.


DENMARK

In Denmark, a traditional Christmas meal on Christmas Eve with content, roast pork, Roasted Goose or Roasted Duck in one of three ways. With the purple potato, cabbage and a large number of pottage. The dessert for the rice pudding, usually put almonds inside, who will be awarded a lucky find gift gifts like almonds. The traditional Christmas drink is grosg's sweet wine and the traditional Christmas beer made especially for the season. The alcohol concentration of these drinks is usually low.

ITALY

Many people have the traditional habit of eating vegetarian food at Christmas. If you don't eat meat, COD can go on the table. Eat shrimp, squid, shellfish meat and fish salad, cucumber fish, eel nut sauce.

GERMANY

The Germans also attached considerable importance to Christmas on Christmas night, people rush home to eat, like Chinese eat a meal. The most famous Christmas food in Germany is the Lebkuchen of the gingerbread. This is a small point between the cake and the biscuit. The traditional gingerbread, with honey and Hu Jiaoli as the material, is sweet and spicy, and the taste is exciting. Now the gingerbread have been modified outside a sprinkling of sugar, not only taste rich, the appearance is quite pleasing.


AMERICA

It is said that the traditional Christmas turkey is derived from the Americas, and the turkey is an indispensable traditional food in the two big days of Thanksgiving and Christmas. In the United States, Christmas dinner is also the same special food cooked corn porridge, above a layer of cream, and put some fruit, sweet and delicious, have a distinctive taste. Some are still in the habit of Christmas dinner table a Roasted Suckling Pig, British and American people also love often put an apple in the pig's mouth, it is said that the custom originated in some big family, because only family can eat a pig, then some people pay attention to the show when the Christmas dinner follow suit.

ENGLAND

The average British family loved to cook a turkey, a large number of Vegetable & Fruit such as carrot, celery, onion, chestnuts, into ten pounds of Turkey, then a variety of surface with spices, then put in the oven baked. The traditional Christmas pudding is very sweet, and a small corner is satisfied! The Christmas pudding is also a traditional food.

AFRICA

When the Christmas Eve bell rings, people can go home for a special Christmas meal. But their Christmas meal is relatively simple, with only some bread, rice, garlic and boiled meat.
