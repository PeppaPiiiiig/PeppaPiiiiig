CANADA

The Christmas dinner in Canada has many similarities with its colonial ancestor, England, and the neighbours of the United States. The traditional Christmas dinner features stuffed turkey (flavoring), mashed potatoes, broth thick juice, cranberry sauce, vegetables, and raisin pudding as a dessert. Egg wine, a kind of milk - base mixed egg juice, sugar and other drinks, usually served with wine, is also very popular in the festival.
